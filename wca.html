About Us

Our family-based furniture company evolved from a Main Street shop to a global brand thanks to the commitment of our employees and the belief that outdoor living is not a luxury - it's a way of life.
We stand by each and every piece of furniture that leaves our factory and guarantee that it can thrive in any type of weather.
From umbrellas to ottomans, nothing we make doesn't have our heart and soul in every single detail.

Our Services

Our curated collections feature responsibly sourced materials, eco-friendly finishes and designs inspired by the way we live - offering both comfort and conscience.

Why Work With Us?

At Canopy Sage, we're more than a furniture company, we are a community of makers and do-ers.

Every piece we build and every project we take on reflects our commitment to craftsmanship and our deep belief in life outdoors.
